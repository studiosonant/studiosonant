:PRODUCTIVE:

Studio Sonant is an independent, high octane, self congratulatory group of sellouts, aiming to become rich while losing their own integrity, and sacrificing all of it for a cup of delicious liquor.

In reality, we're just dudes trying to make games, that bring back the spark we felt as kids, while waiting on load times. We don't really care otherwise, we're not too serious about life, unless we need to argue about nerdy stuff.

PS: Waterworld is a good movie, fight us. 

The lovechild of Ferris Bueller's Day off and Final Fantasy. Creepslore is a hybrid 2.5D point-and-click adventure visual novel with weird characters, unique art, surreal comedy, and just enough text to not make you go crazy. There's also a talking lamp, and is filled with general weirdness.

Our 'flagship title' by which we mean we've been working on it for a while.

A long time.

Too long in fact.

The game came about because we were annoyed about Visual Novels being the way they are. Despite existing as an interesting medium, the genre is plagued by artistic problems, clichés, fetishism, and people pretending to be Japanese. Our goal is to create something different from this norm, and tap into a neglected market, where people can enjoy a game, that leaves a lasting experience, instead of a pair of unzipped pants.

We're also adding in some spice with low poly models, 90's nostalgia, and using the state-of-the-art BSP TECHNOLOGY via building all our backgrounds in the original QUAKE engine.

If you think this sounds interesting, go and hit up our steam page, and wishlist the game. If it doesn't, there's not much we can do, but recommend you some memes.
