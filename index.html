<ABOUT US>

Studio Sonant is an independent game developer based in Europe, fueled by passion towards creative expression. It was founded in 2018 to create unique worlds and experiences that we felt were missing from the market. Our target audience is first and foremost ourselves, and not some make-belief statistic a clueless committee came up with. Ergo: We make the things we want, when we want.

For Business Inqiuires you should contact us through email, for Support and General Information please use the respective Steam & Itch pages or Twitter.

<CREEPSLORE>

The lovechild of Ferris Bueller's Day off and Final Fantasy. Enter a world filled with drunk monsters and their offbeat shenanigans: Birdman's arm got stolen as part of a performance art. Lettuce wants you to eat him. Doctor Scientist rents his body out to a Wound Parasite, and the talking lamp is... well, a talking lamp. Wait- WHAT IS GOING ON HERE?
